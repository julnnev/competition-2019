Who Is Pack&Go?



We are two friends who love to travel and to code! We have worked together on this blog, to share our travel experiences with our friends and family whilst putting our Web Design skills to use. We also enjoy travel photography and love to experience different cultures! We have built this site, in less than 4 days, at the Vodafone #codelikeagirl programme and we look forward to update this site as we discover more places! Follow us on Instagram, Twitter and FaceBook for daily updates!

Happy Travels and Coding!
