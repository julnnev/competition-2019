Bali

  • Dress for the Occasion - Beachwear doesn’t always cut it in Bali – many higher-end bars, restaurants and clubs enforce a dress code.
  • Don't litter! - how equal respect for the beach by not leaving any garbage behind – when the tide comes in, it'll be sucked into the ocean at great cost to the marine ecosystem.
  • Respect Religious Customs

London

  • Stay in central London as you can see much more in less time! - We prefer South Bank as its close to the London Eye and we love being in the center of the action
  • Foresee enough time (at least 3-4 days) and don’t try to see everything
  • Use public transport – use contactless credit card or Oyster card

Paris

  • Paris is a city best explored on foot - get a feel for the authentic Paris and see the settlement from a local’s perspective
  • Learn a few words of French - people are more likely to be helpful if you at least try to make an effort with a little French
  • Visit the Louvre Museum but don’t try and see everything - Make the most of your visit and plan ahead, focusing on one wing or two
